About OmniMedia

The Future of Publishing Starts Here

OmniMedia Syndicated Books was built for a new generation of authors.

The publishing industry has changed. Today's writers need more than editing, formatting, and distribution. They need visibility. They need audience growth. They need marketing systems. They need a team that understands how to position books, brands, and intellectual property in an increasingly digital world.

That's where we come in.

OmniMedia Syndicated Books is a full-service publishing and author development company dedicated to helping writers transform their ideas into professionally published books while creating opportunities for long-term growth and success.

From manuscript development and publishing support to branding, marketing, social media promotion, audiobook production, and global distribution, we provide the tools and resources modern authors need to compete in today's marketplace.

Whether you're publishing your first book or expanding an established catalog, our mission remains the same: To help great stories reach the audiences they deserve.

Our Mission

To empower authors by providing professional publishing, marketing, and audience development solutions that help turn creative ideas into successful publishing ventures.

Our Vision

We believe every author deserves access to professional publishing services, innovative marketing strategies, and growth opportunities that were once available only through traditional publishing houses. Our goal is to help authors maintain creative control while building sustainable careers around their work.

What Makes Us Different

At OmniMedia Syndicated Books, we understand that publishing is only the beginning. That's why our approach goes beyond simply producing books. We help authors:

  • Publish professionally
  • Build their brand
  • Expand their audience
  • Increase visibility
  • Grow their online presence
  • Reach readers worldwide
  • Create new opportunities for their intellectual property

Because in today's publishing landscape, success comes from building an audience—not simply publishing a book.

Our Commitment

Every author's journey is different. That's why we provide personalized support, transparent communication, and professional guidance every step of the way. Whether you're launching your first title or your fifteenth, we're committed to helping you navigate the publishing process with confidence.

Your story matters. Let's share it with the world.
