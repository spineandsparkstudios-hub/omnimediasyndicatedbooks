Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 15, 2026

At OmniMedia Syndicated Books, we are committed to maintaining the trust and confidence of all visitors to our web mainframe. This Privacy Policy details how we handle the personal information and manuscript data you transmit to us.

1. Information We Collect

When you interact with our online forms, we collect personal contact credentials (such as full name, email address, phone number) and proprietary manuscript documents, outlines, or synopses.

2. How We Use Your Data

We process your information to assess your co-publishing candidacy, evaluate potential signing bonuses using our predictive aesthetic models, and establish secure communications.

3. Manuscript Security & Copyright

All submitted work is kept under ironclad digital isolation. We execute strict digital rights verification and do not share your intellectual property with uncertified third parties.
